Social Distancing

  • Know Before You Go
  • Prepare for Transportation
  • Choose Safe Social Activities
  • Keep Distance at Events and Gatherings
  • Stay Distanced While Being Active
  • 6ft distance is a must

Get Yourself Tested

Get yourself tested even in the case of minor symptoms such as:

  • Fever, chills, cough or soar throat
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Muscle or body aches, Headaches
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Congestion or runny nose etc.

Get A Vaccine

During these times of Covid 19 getting a vaccine is critically important not only to protect yourself but also to protect people around you. Get a vaccine as soon as possible.
